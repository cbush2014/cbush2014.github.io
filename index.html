About Me

Hello, My name is Chris R Bush. I am BACK!

When my family moved to Charlotte, I regretfully decided leave a computer programming career and reinvent myself as a commercial real estate appraiser. It sounded great, all your education up front, then you just build upon your experience. While there were many parts of it that I really enjoyed, it was not a good match for me because I am a math/science guy and not a writer.

I just finished an intense code immersion bootcamp learning web development, and am so excited and thankful to spend the rest of my life coding. I love data, numbers, stats, and cool technologies that make life easier for all of us. I really love programming. The best part is that I had over 10 years of experience as a programmer working with Visual Basic and AccessDB. I have dusted off the cobwebs and all the wheels are turning!

I have two high school children, so my hobby list isn't very impressive. I love to cook and experiment with recipes. I am addicted to talk radio and podcasts and learning new technologies. I also enjoy landscaping and fantasy baseball.
